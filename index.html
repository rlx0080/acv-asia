KF-94 Mask

KF94 is one of the grades given to Korean health masks. ‘KF’ stands for ‘Korean Filter’. Korean masks must be certified by the Korea Food & Drug Administration (KFDA) and will be rated KF80/94/99 depending on their performance.1 The numbers quantify the filter performance of the mask.

Nanofibre Filter Mask

Filters made up of Nano-sized fibers have a 99.9% efficiency in trumping viruses and are very comfortable in terms of use. With a thin non-woven fabric, the face masks consist of a very low weight nanofiber coating that fully meets user expectations with its flexible structure and morphology that easily passes moisture.

COVID-19 Test Kit

There are currently 3 types of detection kits are in the market : Molecular, Antigen and Antibody. You can understand the accuracy level : Molecular > Antigen > Antibody. However, each one has its own process and conditions.

Protective Suit

Protective clothing is clothing designed to protect either the wearer's body or other items of clothing from hazards such as heat, chemicals and infection.

Safety Goggles

Goggles, or safety glasses, are forms of protective eyewear that usually enclose or protect the area surrounding the eye in order to prevent particulates, water or chemicals from striking the eyes.
